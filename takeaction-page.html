Take Action

  • The best and most effective way to prevent the environmental destruction caused by animal agriculture is to consume as few animal products as possible. Without the high demand for products such as meat and dairy, less animals would be in existence and many of these environmental problems would be greatly reduced.
  • Take a 22 day vegan challenge to get started!
  • Contact environmental protection agencies and local representatives and encourage them to acknowledge and address the environmental impacts of animal agriculture.
