About

Animal Agriculture is one of the leading causes of environmental destruction, including greenhouse gas emissions, land and water use, habitat loss, rainforest destruction, waste, and ocean dead zones. Despite being the leading cause of these environmental changes, most people are unaware of negatives of animal agriculture. The purpose of this site is to give a brief introduction into these facts, as well as to provide some helpful tips on how to help prevent this environmental damage.
